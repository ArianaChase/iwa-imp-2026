THE ROLE OF BIG TECH AS DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURE

Title THE ROLE OF BIG TECH AS DIGITAL INFRASTRUCTURE
Citation
Credibility
Summary The source claims that the domination Big Tech has on digital infrastructure, essentially monopolizing it, is harmful to society because their business model (profit over accountability and reliability) does not align with the people's well-being. They argue that in democratic, economic, and political aspects this monopolization will only serve to weaken existing institutions and regulations, forcing society to develop a dependency on Big Tech services that are not guaranteed to stay of any certain quality, which may become a danger.

It calls for Denmark and the EU to develop "digital sovereignty," a lack of dependence of its critical digital infrastructure on certain big companies.
